### Tortle stats
* +2 str, -2 dex, +2 wis
* humanoid (tortle)
* size: medium
* movement: 20ft + slow & steady, 30ft. swim 
* x2 slams: 1d4
* can hold breath x10 as long as a human can
* natural armor +2
* survival instinct: +2 Survival & class skill
* languages: Common, Tortle
